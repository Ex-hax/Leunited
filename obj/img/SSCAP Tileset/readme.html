Commissions

This tileset - made for the SRPG Studio Community - was created with the help of numerous patrons and artists who were kind enough to lend a hand and make this happen!

Now that being said, our work isn't done yet. This tileset may be missing a few tiles people want, and that's where you come in.

I'm willing to subsidize any requests or commissions given by the community, so long as you meet these two requirements:

  • Tileset requests must fit the art style used in the current tileset
  • Tiles requested must be something that will be useful to the rest of the community, such as:
    • Asking for additional decorative tiles to complement the current sets
    • Asking for tilesets with different weather conditions (Snow) and environments (Cave, library)

If you have any requests, you can either email me at markyjoe1990@gmail.com, or DM me with my Discord Handle, MarkyJoe#2265

Donations

Any donations given will be used to further subsidize the artists making assets for this tileset.
